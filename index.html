About me

Passionate software developer with a strong software engineering foundation and over two years of collaborative experience at Ericsson.

In age where everyone is trying to find a fully remote job, I am reaching my best productivity levels in office, by networking and collaborating to solve the most challenging problems.

In Ericsson I've contributed to the development of Cloud Native solution for CBRS. As part of a talented team, we developed a Jenkins-based CI/CD pipeline to automate testing for install, upgrade, and rollback scenarios, which reduced bug rates by 25%. I integrated a set of dashbords that provide customers with live monitoring capabolities through a user friendly GUI. I've worked on creating functional, performance, and endurance test plans to esure CBRS reliability in a cloud environment.

Currently I am sharpening my skills in java and python with a focus on security principles. I am looking for a job where I can put my skills to the test and grow further as a professional.
